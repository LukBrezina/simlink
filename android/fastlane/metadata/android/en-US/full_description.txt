SMS for Agents turns your Android phone into an SMS gateway for AI agents you
control. Create an account, pick a SIM to share, and the app gives you a Model
Context Protocol (MCP) URL and token to paste into ChatGPT, Claude, or any
MCP-capable agent. Each agent authenticates to your account, so only you can use
your SIM.

Features:

* Send SMS — agents call a send_sms tool; your phone sends from your chosen SIM.
* Receive SMS — incoming texts are forwarded to your agents.
* Wait for SMS — a push-style tool that blocks until a new text arrives.
* Per-agent tokens, each scoped to a single SIM, revocable at any time.
* Self-hostable server: run your own backend so your messages never touch a
  third party.

This app needs SMS and phone permissions to send and receive on your behalf. It
talks only to the server you connect it to. It is free software.

Note: sending automated messages from a personal SIM may be rate-limited or
blocked by your mobile carrier. Use responsibly and keep volumes low.
